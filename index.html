ABOUT E-CLUB DESIGN

We establish the branding guidelines and visual identity for E-Club and its sub-teams, including posters, logos, web design, as well as other markerting and recruitment materials.

We also run Design & Impact, a competition to bring awareness to social impact on campus through design. Design is creating a more welcoming and easily accessible environment for students of all experiences who are interested in design, whether you are a seasoned designer looking to build your portfolio or a beginner learning how to use design tools like Adobe Creative Suite.
